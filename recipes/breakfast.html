Omllette

Delicious breakfast and healthy as well

Ingredients :

  • 2 EGGS
  • 1-2 SPOONS OF BUTTER
  • 1 SPOON OF MILK
  • SOME SALT
  • SOME PEPPER

How you do it :

  1. Break eggs, mix with a fork, adding water or milk, salt and pepper.
  2. Put a dry frying pan on very low heat, then increase the flame, heat the butter
    (be careful not to brown it), pour in the egg mixture.
  3. Fry uncovered over high heat until the edges of the omelet are set.
  4. Very gently turn to the other side, fry for a while. Serve with side dishes.
